Event

The goal of the conference is simple: to spread great ideas and inspire people to become passionate and open-minded about change in their daily lives.
To build on this vision, our theme is “(In)definite Optimism: Promises for the Future”. From this theme, we strive to tap into what drives McMaster students, Hamilton, and the global community, and what it takes to achieve our dreams and goals. While the only certainty of this day and age is that our individual and collective futures remain uncertain, university students are uniquely positioned to seize and lead lives in unforeseen directions. We hope to interrogate our collective outlook on our personal futures, and how we can craft meaning in our lives and the world we inhabit. To this end, we are inviting speakers and holding workshops centred around how in particular, the vigour of our optimism for the future can impact our personal agency to shape it.

Where?

The Zoetic

526 Concession St, Hamilton, ON L8V 1A6

90 years old but renovated vintage, the Zoetic is Hamilton’s Historical Theatre- reborn. The setting is dark, and intimate, an ideal incubator for the thoughts we hope to emerge at our conference. Look out for theatre seating, a full HD Christie Projector, a 24 foot screen, and full stage lighting. Did we mention that it’s also wheelchair accessible?
