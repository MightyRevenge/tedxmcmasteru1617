Salon Discussion Leader

The role of a discussion leader is to construct a short series of questions to be discussed in small group of delegates at the TEDx McMasterU Salon on October 21 5-6:30 pm. Ideal candidates will have experience with public speaking, socratic circle discussions, and have leadership skills.

Delegate Salon Application

Leading up to the TEDxMcMaster U conference, members of the McMaster community have the opportunity to attend a official TEDx Salon event. Think of a TEDx Salon like a dinner party with the smartest people you know, discussing controversial topics, and developing unique ideas. In a small group setting, a TEDx Salon is meant to spark productive discussions, and allow a more intimate and engaging event.

Based in McMaster University. 1280 Main Street West, Hamilton Ontario, Canada.
This independent TEDx event is operated under license from TED.
